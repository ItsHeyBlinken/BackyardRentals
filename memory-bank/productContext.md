# Product Context

## Why this exists
Backyard Party Rentals is launching as a backyard / party game rental
business in the DFW metroplex. Before going live, the founders need a
public-facing page that:

1. Establishes credibility (so visitors trust submitting their info).
2. Captures interest (a waitlist of warm leads for launch day).
3. Communicates the offering clearly (what they rent, where they serve).

## Target audience
- Parents planning kid birthdays / backyard parties
- School activity coordinators
- Church / youth group leaders
- HR & event coordinators planning corporate / team-building events
- Community / HOA event planners

## Key user goals on this page
- Quickly understand what the business will offer.
- See that it serves *their* city in DFW.
- Sign up to be notified when booking opens.
- (Optional) Share planned event details so the business can prep.

## Tone & voice
- Family friendly, energetic, approachable.
- Premium-but-not-stuffy — a small business that takes itself seriously.
- Clear, plain copy. No jargon.

## Conversion path
Hero CTA → Waitlist form → Success state. The page is intentionally
single-funnel: every CTA points to `#waitlist`.
