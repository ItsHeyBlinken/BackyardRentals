# Project Brief — Backyard Party Rentals (DFW)

## What this is
A pre-launch coming-soon landing page for a backyard party rental business
serving the Dallas Fort Worth (DFW) metroplex.

## Goal
- Make the business look legitimate before launch.
- Build excitement.
- Collect early customer interest (waitlist signups).

## Brand
- **Visible business name:** Backyard Party Rentals (matches the provided logo)
- **Domain (display only, not yet live):** dfwyardgames.com
- **Contact placeholder:** info@dfwyardgames.com
- **Tagline (from logo):** Games. Gatherings. Good Times.

## Service offering (planned)
1. Yard Game Rentals — cornhole, horseshoes, ladder toss, giant connect, etc.
2. NERF Battle Rentals — packages for birthdays, youth groups, team events.
3. Party Packages — bundled multi-game setups.
4. Delivery & Setup — door-to-door, handled for the customer.

## Service area
Dallas, Fort Worth, McKinney, Plano, Frisco, Allen, Richardson, Garland,
plus surrounding North Texas communities.

## Scope of this site (v1)
- Static site: `index.html` + `styles.css` + a single embedded `<script>`.
  No external libraries, frameworks, or CDNs.
- Sections: Header, Hero (Coming Soon), About, Services (4 cards),
  Service Area, Waitlist Form, Footer.
- Front-end-only waitlist form (validated, no backend yet).
- All imagery is the provided `logo.png` plus CSS-generated placeholders.

## Out of scope (v1)
- Backend / database / email capture pipeline.
- Real photography.
- Online booking, payments, inventory, calendar.
- Any analytics or 3rd-party scripts.
